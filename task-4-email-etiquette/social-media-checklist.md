#Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

 Do’s:-
- Use strong and unique passwords  
- Keep your profile professional and updated  
- Verify information before sharing  
- Respect others while communicating online  
- Use privacy settings to protect your data

 Don’ts:-
- Do not share personal information publicly  
- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links  
- Do not post offensive or inappropriate content  
- Do not spread fake news or misinformation  
- Do not overshare your location or daily activities
