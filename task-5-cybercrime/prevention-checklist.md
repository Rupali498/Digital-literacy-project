## Stay Safe Online – Checklist

- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts  
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible  
- Do not click on suspicious links or unknown emails  
- Always verify the authenticity of websites before entering personal details  
- Avoid sharing personal information like phone number, address, or OTPs online  
- Do not share OTP or banking details with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bank  (UPI Safety)  
- Always check UPI payment requests carefully before approving transactions  (UPI Safety)  
- Use secure and private Wi-Fi networks; avoid public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities  
- Regularly update your apps and devices to stay protected from security threats  
- Log out from accounts when using shared or public devices
